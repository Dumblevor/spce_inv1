Invaders 23 - a HTML based video game, emulating the classic Space Invaders.

Credits: Developed by Dimitar, 1 of SEI 23, General Assembly.

Significant help received from Alex, Michael & Nick (a-z).

Instructions:
Kill all alien invaders.

Hit space bar to shoot a laser.
⬅ and ➡ arrow keys navigate ship.
Invaders must not reach your planet.
